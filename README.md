## What It Does

Turns satisfied customers into a steady stream of public reviews, video testimonials, and website-ready social proof — without awkward asking.

Most businesses have happy customers who never leave reviews. This skill closes that gap with a complete system:

1. **Email sequence** that asks at the right moment (3 emails, timed perfectly)
2. **In-app prompts** for SaaS, e-commerce, and mobile apps
3. **Video testimonial kit** — invite email, interview questions, and editing guide
4. **Repurposing engine** — turn one testimonial into 5 social posts + website hero copy + FAQ

- 93% of consumers say reviews influence purchase decisions (BrightLocal)

## Quick Start

1. Install this skill (ClawHub or download from GitHub)
2. Open `prompts/01-email-sequence.md`
3. Fill in your business name, product, and customer outcome
4. Paste into Claude and copy the 3 emails into your email platform
5. Set up the sequence with 3-day and 7-day delays

See `examples/freshbox-review-campaign/` for a complete worked example.

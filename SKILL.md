## Overview

Generates a complete social proof capture system for any business. Turns satisfied customers into public reviews, video testimonials, and website-ready case study quotes — without awkward asking.

Four prompts cover the full social proof flywheel:
1. **Email Review Request Sequence** — 3-email drip optimized for Google, Trustpilot, G2, Yelp, App Store
2. **In-App & Post-Purchase Prompts** — micro-copy for SaaS modals, e-commerce receipts, mobile app push
3. **Video Testimonial Script Guide** — question framework + framing for customer success calls
4. **Social Proof Repurposing Kit** — convert one testimonial into 5 social posts + website hero copy

## Prompts

### Prompt 1: Email Review Request Sequence
**File:** `prompts/01-email-sequence.md`  
**Input:** Business name, product/service, target review platform, customer win/outcome  
**Output:** 3-email sequence (Day 0, Day 3, Day 7) with subject lines, body copy, CTA buttons

### Prompt 2: In-App & Post-Purchase Prompts
**File:** `prompts/02-inapp-prompts.md`  
**Input:** Business type (SaaS/e-commerce/mobile app), trigger moment, desired platform  
**Output:** Modal headline + body + CTA, push notification copy (3 variants), receipt/confirmation page insert

### Prompt 3: Video Testimonial Script Guide
**File:** `prompts/03-video-testimonial.md`  
**Input:** Customer segment, product/service, key outcome they achieved  
**Output:** Pre-call prep email, 7 structured interview questions, recording instructions, "story arc" framework

### Prompt 4: Social Proof Repurposing Kit
**File:** `prompts/04-repurposing-kit.md`  
**Input:** Raw testimonial text, customer name/role/company (optional), business context  
**Output:** 5 social posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Threads), pull quote card copy, hero section copy, FAQ-style objection handler

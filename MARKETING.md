# Marketing Plan: Testimonial & Review Request Generator

## Elevator Pitch

"Every business has happy customers who never leave reviews. This skill fixes that — a complete system to capture reviews, video testimonials, and social proof on autopilot."

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Local business owners, SaaS founders, e-commerce brand managers  
**Secondary:** Marketing agencies managing social proof for clients  
**Tertiary:** Coaches, consultants, freelancers building testimonial pages

**Market size:**
- 33M+ small businesses in the US (all need reviews)
- 9.7M Amazon sellers (all need seller feedback)
- 500K+ SaaS companies on G2/Capterra (all need reviews)
- 73M freelancers (all need testimonial pages)

---

## The Problem (Buyer Pain)

1. **They have happy customers who stay silent.** Ask for reviews? Awkward. Forget to ask? The moment passes.
2. **They don't know when to ask.** Too early = low conversion. Too late = customer forgets.
3. **They don't know how to turn one testimonial into multiple assets.** They post it on their website and stop there — leaving LinkedIn, Instagram, email, and their sales deck empty.
4. **Video testimonials feel impossible to coordinate.** No script, no invite email, no editing guide.

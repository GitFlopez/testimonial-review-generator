### Interview Questions (7)

**Q1:** "Before you found FreshBox, what was meal planning and grocery shopping like for you?"  
*Why it works:* Sets up the "before" state — contrast makes the outcome more powerful  
*Coaching tip:* If they say "stressful," ask "can you give me an example of a typical week?"

**Q2:** "What had you tried before that didn't solve the problem?"  
*Why it works:* Validates that they're not naive — they tried other things and FreshBox won  
*Coaching tip:* If they say "nothing," ask "Did you ever try meal prepping or a different service?"

**Q3:** "What made you decide to give FreshBox a try?"  
*Why it works:* Captures the buying trigger — mirrors the exact moment future buyers are in  
*Coaching tip:* Ask "Was there a specific moment or was it more gradual?"

**Q4:** "What surprised you most after your first few boxes?"  
*Why it works:* Surfaces unexpected value — often the most believable part of the testimonial  
*Coaching tip:* Use silence after their first answer. Count to 3 before responding — they'll usually add more.

**Q5:** "What's the most meaningful change FreshBox has made in your daily routine or family life?"  
*Why it works:* Shifts from product features to life impact — emotional peak of the testimonial  
*Coaching tip:* If they give a vague answer, ask "Can you put a number on it? Hours saved, meals cooked, anything like that?"

**Q6:** "How is your situation different now compared to before you found FreshBox?"  
*Why it works:* Summarizes the transformation — perfect closing soundbite  
*Coaching tip:* "If you had to describe it in one sentence for a friend, what would you say?"

**Q7:** "Who would you most recommend FreshBox to, and why?"  
*Why it works:* Narrows the audience → helps future buyers self-identify. Also provides word-of-mouth framing  
*Coaching tip:* Ask "Is there a specific person in your life you'd forward this to right now?"

---

### Story Arc Framework

**60-Second Cut:**
- 0-5 sec: Open with Q5 answer (the result/outcome — most compelling hook)
- 5-20 sec: Q1 answer (the before state — creates contrast)
- 20-50 sec: Q4 or Q6 answer (the surprise or transformation)
- 50-60 sec: Q7 answer (the recommendation — ends on social proof)

**3-Minute Full Version:**
- 0-15 sec: Hook (Q5 result)
- 15-45 sec: Before state (Q1 + Q2)
- 45-90 sec: Decision moment (Q3)
- 90-150 sec: Discovery and transformation (Q4 + Q5)
- 150-180 sec: Life now + recommendation (Q6 + Q7)

**Lower-Third Text:**
- Name + title: "Maria T., FreshBox Customer (Las Vegas, NV)"
- Result stat: "Saves 3 hrs/week on meal planning"

**End Card CTA:** "Start your first box — freshboxdelivery.com"
